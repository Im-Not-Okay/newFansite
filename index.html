Sleeping With Sirens: Fanpage

Sleeping With Sirens is one of my favorite bands. I love their music, their personality, and just the band all together! My favourite song from Sleeping With Sirens would have to be "Legends." The song is very uplifting, even though the lyrics may seem dark. But as Kelllin said, "It’s a dark record. It sounds dark, but there’s a hopefulness in everything. That’s always something I’ve tried to do as a vocalist. When I write about a sad topic, I always want there to be a light at the end of the tunnel." Learn More...
